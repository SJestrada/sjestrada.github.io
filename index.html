More About Me

I graduated from UC Riverside with a Bachelor's in Creative Writing. Through my college career, I fell in and out of love with the process of creating a story. It was a rocky journey fraut with the pandemic, harsh critiques, reading four different books almost every week, and the occassional insufferable peers. Still, I graduated.

Stories have always intrigued me. They can be modes of escapism and could also ground you in the reality you live in. These aspects are why I believe that writing is so insanely useful for a multitude of jobs.

While I have a particular affinity for writing, I also love to indulge myself in drawing. In my spare time, I write and illustrate graphic novels. So far, my only audience has been my brothers but one day I'd like to expand it to the world.
